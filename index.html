Touhou Project is a bullet hell shoot em' up video game series created by Team Shanghai Project. It was first created in 1995, starting on the PC-98 system. Then in 2002, it made a huge switch to the Windows platform, continuing on Touhou 6: Embodiment of the Scarlet Devil.

As of September 2022, there are 18 mainline games. The most recent game is Touhou 18.5: 100th Black Market, which was released on August 14, 2022 as a commeration for the 100th Comiket Event.

Glossary

  • Danmaku - the romanji for 弾幕, which means "bullet hell" in Japanese.
  • Mukyu (むきゆ)- A fan-popular phrase sometimes said by Patchouli Knowledge, a character from Embodiment of the Scarlet Devil.
  • Gensokyo - the main location used in Touhou Project. It is said to be located in and around the Yatsugatake Mountains in Japan.
  • Fumo (ふも) - plushie recreations of Touhou characters manufactured and sold by GIFT. They are very popular within the fandom and can be bought online through GIFT's website.
  • 1cc (One Credit Clear) - a term frequently used by players of the Touhou games. To accomplish a 1cc run, you must beat a game without having to use an extra credit.

Since a majority of Touhou's fanworks is made by the Japanese community, you can obviously expect to see an overwhelming number of fanworks in Japanese.
